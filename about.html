HISTORY OF MUSINGU BOYS HIGH SCHOOL

The school of choice

Musingu high school was began in 1963 with abunch of missionaries from the Friends church .It began as a missionary school to teach the naive Africans by then how to speak and write english and counting numbers.This was aimed to help them understand the Bible and help them grow in Christianity.The location of the school by then was in Shinyalu constituence western region kenya besides Friend church in Malinya market.It was named after an indigenous three known us the musingu tree in luhya land.Currently the tree is besides new adminstation block.The school became public in 1965 just after independance By then the school could offer o levels certificates with the first principal Mr Laban Ayiro .

The school was promoted to teach up to A level in 1969
The school is located five kilometres from sigalagala center that is on kakamega kisumu road From kakamega take the route towards kisumu ,25 minutes drive to sigalagala take your right toward malinya ,we are located five kilometres from sigalagala polytechnic

The school resides i 200 hectraes of land with forty hectres reserved for Teaches Quater The current building structure at the school is estimated as at 1.2 billion kenya shillings. The school is based on strong christian faith and moral with great association with the friends church.Eventhough of Christian values Musingu Boys apricciate diversifaication and promotes freedom to worship therefore allow students from other religions suchs as muslim and hindus to study and worship freely as long as the religion is not of harm to the other students. Currently the school has a populaton of close to 1300students.
